## 📌 Overview

The **Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. It transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights, helping businesses monitor sales performance, profitability, product performance, and customer payment behavior.

The dashboard was built using **Power Query, Data Modeling, DAX, and interactive visualizations** to provide clear and actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity Sold.
- Analyze monthly and daily sales trends.
- Evaluate product performance.
- Compare category-wise sales.
- Analyze payment methods.
- Support data-driven business decisions.
